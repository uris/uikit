            # Funnel Sans
            [Funnel Sans](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Funnel+Sans/about?query=funnel+sans) is a modern, variable weight, sans-serif typefaces with both clarity and character, originally developed by [NORD ID](https://fonts.google.com/?query=NORD%20ID) and [Kristian Möller](https://fonts.google.com/?query=Kristian%20Möller) for Funnel. The typefaces are inspired by the movement and shapes of data points.

            Funnel Sans is a functional yet personal sans-serif, featuring both square and circular shapes in its letterforms. It's legibility and clarity in all sizes, together with it's character, gives experiences a unique and personal touch.
